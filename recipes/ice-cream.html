Ice cream

Description

Welcome to our Easy No-Churn Ice Cream recipe page, where we're about to embark on a delightful journey into the realm of homemade frozen treats! Say goodbye to complicated churners and hello to simplicity with our no-churn method. With just a few basic ingredients and minimal effort, you'll soon be savoring creamy, dreamy scoops of your favorite flavors right at home. From classic vanilla to indulgent chocolate chip cookie dough or refreshing strawberry swirl, the possibilities are endless. Join us as we uncover the magic of creating luscious, velvety ice cream without the hassle. Get ready to chill out and indulge in a spoonful of frozen bliss!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chilled heavy cream
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Add heavy cream to the bowl of a stand mixer with a whisk attachment. Beat on medium-high speed until stiff peaks form, 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Add sweetened condensed milk, vanilla and salt. Mix on low speed until fully combined, 20 to 30 seconds, scraping sides of bowl as needed.
  4. Transfer mixture into a 9x5-inch loaf pan, spreading evenly in the pan. Cover with plastic wrap, gently pressing down to touch the ice cream mixture and seal out any air bubbles. Freeze until firm, at least 6 hours before serving.
  5. Enjoy!
